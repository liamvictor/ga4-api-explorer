Digital Marketing Perspective - Missing Features:

1.  **Monetization / E-commerce:**
    *   Revenue Tracking: No reports for purchase revenue, total revenue, or average order value (AOV).
    *   Item Performance: Reports for specific item sales, quantity, and item-level conversion rates.
    *   Shopping Stages: Analysis of the "E-commerce Journey" (view_item -> add_to_cart -> begin_checkout -> purchase).

2.  **Conversion Optimization (CRO):**
    *   **Generic Conversions:** A report for ALL Key Events (formerly Conversions) by source/medium, not just specific events like 'generate_lead'.
    *   **On-Site Search:** Analysis of search queries performed on the site to identify content gaps.
    *   **Form Analytics:** Tracking form start vs. completion rates.

3.  **Paid Media (Google Ads):**
    *   Integration of Google Ads specific dimensions (Campaign Name, Ad Group, Keyword) to measure ROAS and CPA within the tool.

4.  **Audience & Retention:**
    *   **Cohort Analysis:** A proper retention table showing how users return week-over-week or month-over-month.
    *   **Demographics:** Age and Gender distribution (requires Google Signals enabled).

5.  **Experience & Engagement:**
    *   **Outbound Click Tracking:** Identifying where users are going when they leave the site.
    *   **File Downloads:** Tracking PDF/Asset engagement.
    *   **Attribution Comparison:** Comparing performance across different attribution models (e.g., Last Click vs. Data-Driven).
